My Work

EKO

EKO is an innovative platform that connects the general public with repair technicians, enabling the sale of unusable electronic and electrical components at better prices. This platform helps technicians find parts easily, offering affordable repair services while promoting eco-friendly practices, upcycling, and contributing to a circular economy.

Blog App

The Blog App is a web application designed to explore and share content across a variety of fascinating topics, including art, science, technology, cinema, food, and more. Built using modern full-stack technologies, the app allows users to create, read, update, and delete blog posts, interact with others through comments, and browse through categories of interest.

Orion

Orion is a next-generation collaboration app designed to streamline team communication and project management. Built with Flutter and Firebase, it offers features like group chats, automated meeting minutes, to-do lists, and push notifications, ensuring teams stay organized and connected.

Object Tracking

This object tracking project utilizes two computer vision techniques, KCF and CSRT, to track objects in video frames. By comparing their speed and accuracy, the project aims to evaluate which algorithm performs better in terms of efficiency and precision, providing insights into their effectiveness for real-time applications.

YouTube Channel Analysis

This Python project leverages the YouTube Data API to extract and analyze data from YouTube channels and their videos. The extracted data is loaded into a Pandas DataFrame for analysis, allowing for comparisons between multiple channels based on their statistics.

FloodGuard

Flood Guard is a predictive modeling project that assesses flood risk in the U.S. based on current rainfall conditions. By leveraging historical weather data and machine learning techniques, it forecasts potential flooding events, offering valuable insights for disaster management and response efforts.
