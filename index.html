Thrissur Digital Litearcy

2023-24

I completed an internship with the Thrissur Corporation, working on a digital literacy project aimed at educating people unfamiliar with modern technology. Our team created detailed slides and documents in both English and Malayalam to help individuals like elderly people, students, and workers learn how to use essential digital tools such as Google Pay, YouTube, and PhonePe. The project focused on making technology more accessible to those who had little to no experience with smartphones and online services. To ensure clarity, we designed the materials in a simple and structured way, covering step-by-step instructions. We frequently visited the municipal corporation, interacting with officials and understanding the specific challenges faced by different groups. A major highlight of this internship was that most of our class participated, working together to make a real impact. It was a rewarding experience, allowing us to contribute to bridging the digital divide while gaining valuable insights into real-world problem-solving and community engagement.
